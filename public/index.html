本工具是 GPT 句子分級模組的互動展示介面，協助你理解句子的語氣結構與風險等級。
點擊下方任一句子，即可即時分析 AI 輸出的語氣屬性，並顯示以下四項指標：

✔︎ 語氣分級（A–D）：根據語句的內容分類為：
‣ A 級（事實）— 可查證、具明確來源。
‣ B 級（推論）— 合理預測或邏輯判斷。
‣ C 級（主觀）— 帶有個人觀點、情感或假設語氣。
‣ D 級（幻想）— 虛構敘事、假想情境或創作式語句。

✔︎ 分級解釋：說明模型為何做出該分級。
✔︎ 幻覺風險：分析語句是否具 AI 幻覺傾向（高 / 中 / 低）。
✔︎ 使用建議：提供語句可能幻覺／失真的方向，以提升判斷基準。

本系統由 Erica Vega 團隊設計，致力於推廣 GPT 使用者的語氣素養（Tone Literacy）與敘事理解力。


This tool is an interactive demo of GPT-based sentence tone classification, designed to help you understand the tone structure and hallucination risk of AI-generated outputs.
Click any sentence below to receive an immediate tone analysis across the following four dimensions:

✔︎ Tone Grade (A–D): Each sentence is categorized based on its expression style:
‣ A (Factual) — Verifiable statements with identifiable sources.
‣ B (Inferential) — Logical predictions or reasonable assumptions.
‣ C (Subjective) — Opinions, emotional phrasing, or hypothetical tone.
‣ D (Fictional) — Creative narratives, imagined scenarios, or storytelling language.

✔︎ Explanation: Describes why the model assigned the specific tone grade.
✔︎ Hallucination Risk: Evaluates the likelihood of AI hallucination (High / Medium / Low).
✔︎ Suggestion: Provides revision ideas to identify or reduce hallucinated or distorted phrasing.

Developed by the Erica Vega team to promote tone literacy and improve user awareness of GPT output structures and credibility.
